sourabh 06/26/2022 2:04 PM
I think 3a and 4 are related so I'll try to provide a bit of clarity here: Both the lend method in Lender.sol and the redeem method are overloaded in Redeemer.sol. Users will call Illuminate's redeem method to redeem their debt. In other words, the non-Illuminate redeem calls transfer the principal tokens from the lender contract to the redeemer contract and then retrieve the underlying asset from their respective protocol. From there, Illuminate's redeem burns the user's iPTs, and in turn sends the user the underlying asset. The redemption process is a two step process with the overloaded redeem method getting called twice.
